
Word: djedi
Rafsi: dje, dei
Definition: x1 is x2 full days in duration (default is 1 day) by standard x3; (adjective:) x1 is diurnal.

Notes:
Today (= {cabdei}); tomorrow (= {bavlamdei}); yesterday (= {prulamdei}).  See also {donri}, {detri}, {jeftu}, {masti}, {nanca}, {nicte}, {tcika}.
