
benji - bej, be'i
x1 transfers/sends/transmits x2 to receiver x3 from transmitter/origin x4 via means/medium x5.

Also possibly "sharing"; no (complete) alienation from origin is implied.  x5 carrier.  See also {muvdu}, {dunda}, {mrilu}, {nirna}, {xruti}, {cradi}, {tivni}, {preja}, cmavo list {be'i}, {bevri}, {mrilu}, {tcana}.
