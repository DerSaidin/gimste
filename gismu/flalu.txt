
Word: flalu
Rafsi: fla
Definition: x1 is a law specifying x2 (state/event) for community x3 under conditions x4 by lawgiver(s) x5.

Notes:
$x_1$ is a legality; $x_2$ is legal/licit/legalized/a legality (= {selfla} for reordered places).  See also {javni}, {ritli}, {zekri}, {pulji}, {tinbe}.
