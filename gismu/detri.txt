
Word: detri
Rafsi: det
Definition: x1 is the date [day,{week},{month},year] of event/state x2, at location x3, by calendar x4.

Notes:
(time units in $x_1$ are specified as numbers separated by pi'e or are unit values massified with joi); See also cmavo list {de'i}, {djedi}, {jeftu}, {masti}, {nanca}, {tcika}.
