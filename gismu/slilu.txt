
Word: slilu
Rafsi: sli
Definition: x1 oscillates at rate/frequency x2 through set/sequence-of-states x3 (complete specification).

Notes:
Also (expressible either with desku or slilu): side to side, to and fro, back and forth, reciprocal (motion), rotates, revolves.  See also {dikni}, {rilti}, {morna}, {desku}, {janbe}, {boxna}.
